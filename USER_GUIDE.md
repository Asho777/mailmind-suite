# MailMind Suite - User Operating Instructions

Welcome to **MailMind Suite**, your premium, AI-powered email management dashboard. This guide covers every feature, setting, and option available to help you master your communication workflow.

---

## 1. Dashboard & Navigation
The sidebar provides quick access to all core modules:
*   **Inbox**: Your primary communication hub.
*   **AI Queue**: Real-time view of emails currently being analyzed or drafted by the AI.
*   **Handled**: Archive of emails resolved by the AI or yourself.
*   **Review Needed**: High-priority items that require your manual approval before the AI can proceed.
*   **Analytics**: In-depth reporting on response times, AI efficiency, and sentiment analysis.
*   **Automation**: The "brain" of the app where you configure AI behaviors and rules.
*   **Contacts**: Your relationship database with full import/export capabilities.
*   **Templates**: Reusable, variable-based message blueprints.

---

## 2. Managing Emails
### Reading & Responding
*   **Selection**: Click any email in a list to view its full content in the detail pane.
*   **AI Summary**: AI automatically generates summaries for long threads (top of the email view).
*   **Quick Actions**: On hover, use the star icon to prioritize or the trash icon to delete.
*   **Snoozing**: Temporarily hide emails until you're ready to deal with them.

### Composing (New Message)
*   **Recipients**: Type an address or select one from your **Contacts**.
*   **Attachments**: Use the "paperclip" icon to select files from your local storage.
*   **Emoji Picker**: Click the "smiley" icon to access a grid of professional emojis.
*   **Templates**: Click the "document" icon to instantly inject a pre-written template.
*   **Scheduling**: Under the "..." menu, select **Schedule Send** to delay delivery.

---

## 3. Automation Centre
This is where you define how the AI acts on your behalf.
*   **Mode Selection**:
    *   **Ghost Mode**: AI drafts responses but stays hidden (Manual Send).
    *   **Co-Pilot**: AI suggests actions and drafts for your review.
    *   **Autopilot**: AI handles everything based on your rules (Full Automation).
*   **Confidence Threshold**: Use the slider to set the AI's "certainty" level. If AI is less confident than this %, it will always ask for review.
*   **Rule Management**:
    *   **Create Rule**: Define triggers (e.g., "Contains 'Invoice'") and actions (e.g., "Forward to Billing").
    *   **Toggle/Edit**: Use the "Play/Pause" icon to enable/disable rules instantly.

---

## 4. Contacts & Relationships
*   **Adding Contacts**: Click "Add Contact" for a full-detail form (Phone, Company, Address).
*   **Priority (Star)**: Highlight key clients to ensure they get faster AI processing.
*   **Import/Export**:
    *   **Export**: Save your entire database as a `.json` backup.
    *   **Import**: Restore from a MailMind backup or sync from Gmail, Outlook, or Thunderbird.
    *   **De-duplication**: The system automatically skips duplicate emails during import.

---

## 5. Templates
*   **Variables**: Use `{{name}}` or `{{date}}` in your body text. The AI will replace these with real data when used.
*   **Usage Tracking**: The lightning bolt icon shows how many times a template has been used.
*   **Copy to Clipboard**: Use the "Copy" icon to take a template outside the app.

---

## 6. Analytics & Reporting
*   **Performance Metrics**: Track "Emails Handled by AI" vs "Human Input".
*   **Sentiment Analysis**: Visual charts showing the emotional tone of your incoming mail.
*   **Export Reports**: Click "Download Full Report" to generate a professional PDF/CSV summary for the selected time range (7D, 30D, etc.).

---

## 7. Settings & Security
*   **System Status**: Monitor AI Health, Database Connection, and API Latency.
*   **AI Configuration**: Adjust the core model behavior and response style.
*   **Theme**: Toggle between dark and glassmorphism themes (System Default).
